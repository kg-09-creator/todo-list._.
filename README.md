# todo-list._.

**Link:** https://kg-09-creator.github.io/todo-list._./
- This is basically a to-do list that lets you add tasks according to priority along with a Pomodoro timer and music to help users focus.

# **How to Use**
- **Adding Tasks**
- 1) Type in a task in the "Enter a new task" box.
- 2) Then, you can use the dropdown menu that says "Low" to set the priority of the task based how urgent/important it is compared to other tasks. 
- 3) Then either hit the "Enter" key or click "Add Task" to add the task. You don't have to enter tasks in order of priority as they will be automatically sorted in order of the priority they are assigned(Red for high priority, yellow for medium, and green for low. They'll be sorted kind of like a traffic light with red at the top and green at the bottom).
- 4) When you complete a task, either click on the task's name to cross it out, which will mark it as done but allow you to see the completed task, or click the "x" to delete it entirely.

- **Pomodoro Timer**
- 1) Just click start to begin the 25-minute timer and reset when you want to reset the timer. When finished, it should play a soft alarm sound when it's time to take a break.

-**Music**
- As of now, there are three available lofi tracks you can play. Click on either of the three buttons to try them out - Lofi Beats, Deep Focus, or Rainy Night.
- **note: sometimes when the webpage is first opened, the music box says "This live stream recording is not available." Not sure why that happens, but the videos do work, and you can load them easily by clicking one of the three music buttons below the box.**

# Inspiration 
- I procrastinate a lot and it can sometimes be hard for me to focus on studying for longer periods of time. Things like keeping track of what you have to do, using Pomodoro methods, and sometimes listening to music without lyrics are all things that can help one focus better, so I decided to combine all of these things on one webpage to minimize distractions from switching tabs.
